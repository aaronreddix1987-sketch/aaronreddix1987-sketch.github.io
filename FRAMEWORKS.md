# TTI WEBSITE UPGRADE — FRAMEWORK RESEARCH

## Dan Martell (SaaS Academy / Buy Back Your Time)
- **Rocket Demo Builder**: Pain → Current State → Dream State → Demo → Close
- **Chat Sales**: Built 8-figure business without sales calls — chat-based closing
- **Challenge Funnel**: 5-day challenge → activation → reduce churn → upsell
- **Buyback Principle**: Delegate low-value tasks, focus on $10K/hr activities
- **Sales Pipeline**: SDR → AE → CS → AM (4 roles, AI replaces SDR entirely)
- **Key Copy**: "We help [target] get [result] in [timeframe] without [pain]"
- **Visual Style**: Clean, navy blue, professional, glass office aesthetic

## Alex Hormozi ($100M Offers / $100M Leads)
- **Value Equation**: Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
- **Grand Slam Offer**: Stack bonuses, reduce risk, increase perceived value
- **Lead Magnets**: 3 types — Reveal a Problem, Give a Sample, Give a Tool
- **Guarantee Stack**: Unconditional + Conditional + Anti-Guarantee
- **Price Anchoring**: Show the cost of NOT buying (lost revenue, wasted time)
- **Scarcity + Urgency**: Limited spots, deadline, cohort-based
- **Key Copy**: "Make offers so good people feel stupid saying no"

## Grant Cardone (10X / Cardone University)
- **10X Rule**: 10X your targets, 10X your actions
- **Close from Seat**: Always present seated, never standing
- **10 Commandments of Sales**: Be sold on your offer, know your value prop, always agree
- **Persistence**: Ask again and again — people say yes after being worn down
- **Key Copy**: "Success is your duty, obligation, and responsibility"
- **Urgency**: "Every deal you don't close costs you $X per day"

## Russell Brunson (ClickFunnels / Perfect Webinar)
- **Perfect Webinar Framework**: 
  1. The One Thing (Big Domino)
  2. 3 Secrets (break false beliefs)
  3. Stack Slide (show all value)
  4. Close with urgency
- **Funnel Structure**: Opt-in → Thank You → Webinar → Application → Sales Call
- **Value Ladder**: Free → Low Ticket → Mid Ticket → High Ticket → Continuity
- **Hook-Story-Offer**: Every page follows this structure
- **Stack Slide**: List everything they get, show total value, then reveal price

## Sam Ovens (Consulting.com)
- **Webinar Funnel**: Facebook Ads → Landing Page → Webinar → Application → Call
- **Promise**: "How to get 30-50 high-ticket clients per month"
- **Case Study Proof**: Before/after screenshots, revenue screenshots
- **Application Funnel**: Qualify leads before the call — only talk to serious buyers
- **Key**: Long-form VSL (Video Sales Letter) with case studies embedded

## COMBINED FRAMEWORK FOR TTI WEBSITE REBUILD

### Structure (Hook → Story → Offer → Close):
1. **HERO**: Interrupt pattern + Big bold promise + Social proof numbers
2. **PROBLEM AGITATION**: Show the pain of NOT having AI (lost leads, wasted money)
3. **CASE STUDIES**: Before/after results with specific numbers
4. **THE MECHANISM**: Explain the 48-hour AI loop (the "secret")
5. **GRAND SLAM OFFER STACK**: Everything they get + bonuses + value stack
6. **GUARANTEE**: Risk reversal — "If we don't book you X demos, you don't pay"
7. **URGENCY/SCARCITY**: Limited spots, deadline, cohort-based
8. **LEAD CAPTURE**: Application-style form (qualifies leads)
9. **SOCIAL PROOF TICKER**: Live deals closing in real-time
10. **CONTACT**: Direct CEO access (Martell-style personal touch)
